To change a cloud server's root/admin password from your account's list of cloud servers:

  1. Login to the Cloud Control Panel
  2. In the list of servers, locate the name of the server you wish to change.
  3. At the left of server's name, click the cog to open a list of availiable actions.
  4. From the drop-down menu, click Change Password to open space to enter the password.
  5. In the blank space under New Root Admin Password, type the new password and click Change Password.
