NOTE: This article refers to the Cloud Sites Control Panel. You can access this interface from the Cloud Control Panel by clicking your Rackspace Cloud account username in the upper-left corner of the control panel and selecting Cloud Sites.

This article will walk you through accessing your bandwidth and other Cloud Sites usage reports via the control panel.

  1. Log in to the Cloud Sites Control Panel.
  2. Click on Your Account.
  3. Click on Reports.

  4. Under Usage Data you will see information about bandwidth used as well as the total amount of disk space you are using. Also available on that screen is a chart showing resource use over time.
