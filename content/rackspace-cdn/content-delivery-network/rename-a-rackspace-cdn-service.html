To rename a service in Rackspace CDN, follow these steps:

1. Access Rackspace CDN as described in Access Rackspace CDN.

2. In the list of CDN services, click the gear icon beside the service that you want to rename and select Rename Service.

3. In the popup dialog box, enter the new name for the service and then click Rename Service.
