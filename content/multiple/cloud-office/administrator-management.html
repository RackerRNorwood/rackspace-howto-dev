From the Administrators section of the control panel, you can manage your administrators' access and activity.

You can add multiple administrators with varying degrees of account access. There are Super, Standard, and Limited administrators. Limited administrators only have access to specifically assigned sections of the control panel. You can also monitor and export logs of all administrator logins and actions.
