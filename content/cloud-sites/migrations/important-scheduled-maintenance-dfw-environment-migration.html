Overview

We are pleased to inform you that in order to better serve your Cloud Sites offering, Rackspace made a significant investment in new infrastructure within our Dallas-Fort Worth (DFW) datacenter. With this scheduled migration, some customers may experience brief downtime. With continued ServiceNet connectivity, this migration allows for upgraded hardware and software versions, deployment of new features, as well as lays the groundwork for future enhancements.

Timeframe

The scheduled maintenance will commence in mid-Q3 with an anticipated completion of Q4 of 2015.
