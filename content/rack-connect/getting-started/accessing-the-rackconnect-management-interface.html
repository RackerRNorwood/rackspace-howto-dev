You can access the RackConnect Management Interface from the MyRackspace Portal.  To view your RackConnect configuration, click Network > RackConnect.

On the RackConnect page, you can add a Cloud account to your RackConnect configuration, view public IP addresses for your connected Cloud Servers, set network policies for your Cloud Servers, and view the progress of all automation.

The interface displays the servers on your RackConnect account with colored status indicators.

The status indicators can be green, blue, red, or yellow.

  • Green circle:  Deployed
  • Blue circle:  Deploying
  • Red circle:  Failed
  • Yellow circle:  Removing
