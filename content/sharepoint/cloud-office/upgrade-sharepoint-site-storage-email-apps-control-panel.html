The SharePoint service provides 250 MB of storage at no charge. When you are ready to upgrade, you can do so in 1 GB increments in your control panel.

To upgrade SharePoint storage, log in to the control panel, and perform the following steps:

 

  1. At the top of the page click the Add Storage link Beneath the Microsoft SharePoint.

  2. In the Quantity field, enter the amount of additional storage that you want in whole numbers. Your current monthly rate, along with your new monthly rate, and prorated charges are displayed in the Summary section.

  3. Click Review Order.
  4. Review the upgrade details and billing information. To update billing information click Edit.
  5. Click Complete Order.
    Note: Prorated charges will appear on your next bill.
