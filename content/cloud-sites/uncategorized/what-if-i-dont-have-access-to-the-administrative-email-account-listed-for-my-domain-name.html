--- node_id: 711 title: "What if I don't have access to the administrative email account listed for my domain name?" permalink: article/what-if-i-dont-have-access-to-the-administrative-email-account-listed-for-my-domain-name type: article created_date: '2011-03-16 21:57:40' created_by: RackKCAdmin last_modified_date: '2011-09-07 16:5836' last_modified_by: matt.wheeler products: Cloud Sites categories: Uncategorized body_format: tinymce ---

You must have access to the administrative email to approve transfer requests when you are changing registrars or updating your name servers. Your current registrar will send an email to this account that will require a reply from you, confirming that the transfer is authorized. If you do not have access to this email address, please contact customer support for help.

You can also do a WHOIS search to find the administrative email and the current registar for a domain. See How do I do a WHOIS search? for more information.
