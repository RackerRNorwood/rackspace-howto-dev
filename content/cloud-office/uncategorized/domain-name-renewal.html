Do you have your domain name hosted with Rackspace and need to renew it? No problem! It's simple - we make it easy for you to perform this procedure right through your control panel; let's review the steps below to get your domain name with Rackspace renewed: 

1. First thing you'll want to do is login to your control panel at cp.rackspace.com and mouse over the Go to section drop-down menu and select Domains.

2. Next, we'll click the DNS Settings link under the Manage section.

3. Search for the domain name that you want to renew and click the Renew link in the Expiration Date column.

4. In the Extend registration for drop-down menu, select the number of years and click the Renew button.

Note: If you need to change your billing information, click the Edit link on the left next to Payment Information; make any changes necessary and Save.
